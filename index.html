About Dr. R. Hima Spoorthi

With over 12 years of clinical excellence, Dr. R. Hima Spoorthi leads Ashwini Dental Clinic with a mission to deliver gentle, precise, and lasting dental solutions. Graduating with honors from Panineeya Institute of Dental Sciences, Hyderabad, she has since advanced her skills in implants, cosmetic dentistry, and pediatric care.

Dr. Spoorthi believes in a holistic approach — treating not just the tooth, but the patient. Her warm demeanor and commitment to pain-free procedures have earned her the trust of families across Miyapur.

Why Patients Love Her

  • Child-Friendly Care: No fear, just fun!
  • Advanced Tech: Digital X-rays, 3D imaging, laser dentistry
  • Same-Day Services: Implants, crowns, whitening
