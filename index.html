About Ashwini Dental Clinic

Ashwini Dental Clinic, led by the esteemed Dr. Hima Spoorthi, is dedicated to providing exceptional dental care in a comfortable and compassionate environment. We believe in a patient-centric approach, focusing on individualized treatment plans that meet your unique oral health needs and aesthetic goals.

Our clinic is equipped with state-of-the-art technology, ensuring precise diagnostics and effective treatments. From routine check-ups and cleanings to advanced cosmetic dentistry and restorative procedures, Dr. Spoorthi and her team are committed to delivering outstanding results that enhance your smile and overall well-being.

We prioritize patient education, ensuring you are fully informed about your oral health and treatment options. Experience the difference of personalized care at Ashwini Dental Clinic. We look forward to welcoming you and helping you achieve a healthy, beautiful smile.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are your clinic hours?

Our clinic hours are Monday to Saturday, from 9:00 AM to 7:00 PM. We are closed on Sundays. Appointments are highly recommended to ensure you receive timely care.

Do you accept dental insurance?

Yes, we accept a wide range of dental insurance plans. Please contact our front desk with your insurance details, and we'll be happy to verify your coverage and benefits.

What safety measures do you have in place for COVID-19?

Patient and staff safety is our top priority. We adhere to the highest standards of sterilization and cross-infection control. This includes extensive disinfection protocols, HEPA air filtration, and personal protective equipment (PPE) for our team. We also practice social distancing in waiting areas.

How often should I visit the dentist?

We generally recommend dental check-ups and cleanings every six months for most patients. However, based on your oral health needs, Dr. Spoorthi might recommend more frequent visits.

What should I do in a dental emergency?

In case of a dental emergency, please call our clinic immediately at 040-12345678. If it's after hours, our answering service will guide you on the next steps. Do not hesitate to reach out for urgent care.

Are you accepting new patients?

Yes, Ashwini Dental Clinic is always happy to welcome new patients to our practice. We strive to make your first visit as smooth and informative as possible. You can book your first appointment online or by phone.

What kind of payment options do you offer?

We accept various payment methods including cash, major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, Amex), and debit cards. For extensive treatments, we can discuss flexible payment plans or financing options to ensure you get the care you need.
