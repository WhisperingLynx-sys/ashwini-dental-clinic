Frequently Asked Questions

What payment options do you accept?

We accept most major credit cards, debit cards, and cash. We also work with various dental insurance providers and offer flexible payment plans to ensure dental care is accessible for everyone. Please contact our front desk for more detailed information regarding your specific insurance plan or payment arrangements.

How often should I visit the dentist?

For most patients, we recommend a dental check-up and cleaning every six months. Regular visits help us monitor your oral health, prevent common issues like cavities and gum disease, and address any concerns early on. Your dentist may recommend more frequent visits based on your individual needs and oral health history.

Do you offer emergency dental services?

Yes, we understand that dental emergencies can happen at any time. We strive to accommodate emergency appointments as quickly as possible. If you are experiencing a dental emergency, please call our clinic immediately, and we will guide you through the next steps and schedule your urgent visit.

What steps do you take for patient safety?

Patient safety is our top priority. We adhere to the highest standards of sterilization and infection control protocols as mandated by dental associations. Our equipment is regularly sanitized, and disposable tools are used whenever appropriate. We maintain a clean and safe environment for all our patients and staff.
